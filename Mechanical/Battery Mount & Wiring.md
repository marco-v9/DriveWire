## Battery Mount and Wiring

Mounted the 4 AA battery pack to the underside of the chassis for lower centre of gravity. Battery pack sits between rear and front wheels for best balance. 

Power wiring has been routed through chassis openings, with zip ties added for strain relief on joints and cable management.
